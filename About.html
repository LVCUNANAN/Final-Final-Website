About Me

VMT Studio is a website dedicated to my various artworks, as well as my freelance business of taking commissioned art pieces. I am known as @veemadethis on other social media networks, and I have been making art as long as I can remember. Creating digital art is one of my passions growing up, and I like to explore various genres in art such as character design, portrait making, and even storyboarding. The hobby stemmed from a simple habit of tracing cartoon pictures and eventually grew into a skill of bringing ideas to life through my own drawings. There are various influences to my interest in visual arts, of which came from several different media genres such as video games, movies, TV shows (most notably animated TV shows), fantasy books, board games, and more

I started posting my artworks on social media during the pandemic era. In time, I also opened for commissions. It was a success, although it was hard to maintain communication through social media direct messages. This inspired me to create a website for both my art and for accepting commissions, wherein the purchasing process will be a smoother process as it is directed towards a business account. It also helps keep track of purchase history as compared to searching through direct messages.

My art style is a mix of semi-realism and stylized cartoon. I like to play around with colors and lighting to create a mood in my art pieces, and I am always open to learning new techniques to improve my craft. I am also open to collaborations, so if you have any inquiries, feel free to contact me through the Contact page. And if you want to commission an artwork, check out the Commission page!
