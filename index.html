VMT Studios is a website dedicated to the various artworks made by the hobbyist digital artist @veemadethis. Come and explore the website to see the different art that I have made over the years!

Digital Illustrations

Making digital art is a fun creative outlet. Similar to traditional art, you use a pen and paper, which in my case, is a graphic tablet and stylus to bring my ideas to life. It takes trial and error to get the hang of it, but once you do, the possibilities are endless. Creativity, inspiration, and dedication are the masterminds behind the process of creating your own digital illustration.

Multimedia Visual Art

Visual art in general is pleasing to the eyes. Visual art in various medias such as video games, movies, TV shows, and even music videos, that were built with love and care on the other hand are absolute masterpieces. It may not be easy to recreate them, but the care and thought put into creating these pieces can definitely inspire one's creativity.
